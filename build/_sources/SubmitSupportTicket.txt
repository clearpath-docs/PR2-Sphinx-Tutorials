Submit a Support Ticket
=========================

`Click here <http://willowgarage.zendesk.com/>`_ to submit a support ticket through Zendesk

When submitting a ticket, please provide the following information: 

*  Describe exactly what you were doing or are trying to do, and exactly what, if anything, went wrong.
*  Names and versions of stacks/packages that you're using.
*  Your platform (architecture, OS & version/distro).  For Linux, always provide the distro and kernel versions.
*  Any warnings or errors. Cut and paste them directly from the terminal window to which they were printed.
